TYPOGRAPHY

The website will feature a smart combination of typefaces designed to compliment each other, be readable, and beautiful. Paragraphs will use the classic font, Baskerville, a serif font with a rich trdition dating back to the Renaissance. Headlines will use the modern sans-serif, Montserrat, which is easy to read and attractive.
