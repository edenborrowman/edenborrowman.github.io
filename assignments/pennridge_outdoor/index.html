Article Title

When you're ready to get off the beaten path, Southeastern Idaho is a visitor's paradise. With spectacular camping sites, endless trails for biking and hiking, streams for fishing, rivers for rafting, and rocks for climbing, The Gem State is an outdoor wonderland. Here is Penridge Outdoor's Top 5 list of southern Idaho outdoor activities:

1. Stargazing in Craters of the Moon National Monument
2. Rock Climbing in City of Rocks National Reserve
3. Boating in Bear Lake
4. Biking through Thousand Springs Scenic Byway
5. Rafting on the Snake River
