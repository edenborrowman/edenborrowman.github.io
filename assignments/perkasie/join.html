Join Us!

Welcome to the Perkasie Borough Chamber of Commerce inquiry page. We are happy to know of your interest in joining our association. Please fill out the following form and you will be contacted by a member of the onboarding committee within five business days.

Joining the Perkasie Borough Chamber of commerce gives you access to a thriving, vibrant community of like-minded business owners and citizens. Additionally, your membership level will provide benefits to help your business grow. All levels are invited to the monthly roundtable for training, lectures, and networking opportunities. There are additional quarterly activities at the Bronze and Silver levels, and Gold members enjoy a yearly one-on-one business plan review with a Chamber expert.

Members also have access to steeply discounted legal services, HR support, advertising consultation, graphic design, and web development packages. The Chamber has a box at Citizens Bank Park and Phillies tickets are available at a discounted price commensurate with your membership level.

Contact us today for a more complete description of what the Chamber can do for you!
